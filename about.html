Our story

From a one-room chamber to India's idea-keepers.

Founder Arjun Menon left a tier-one litigation practice after watching a hardware startup lose its core invention to a faster filer. He opened a single room on 100ft Road with one belief: protection should be quick, clear and affordable for the people actually building things.

Today, under the SNAB Solutions group, the firm runs eleven patent and trademark attorneys across Bengaluru, with clients from garage-stage founders to listed manufacturers. We still answer the phone, still quote in plain rupees, and still treat every idea as if it were our own.

What we stand on

Three rules we never file without.

Nine years and 2,400 matters later, these are the only three that have never changed.

Rule 01
◷

Speed wins races

In IP the date stamp decides everything. We file fast, search the same week, and never let a priority date slip.

Rule 02
≡

Plain language

No Latin walls. We explain claims, classes and risk in words a founder can repeat to their board.

Rule 03
₹

Honest rupees

Fixed fees wherever possible, quoted upfront. You'll never open an invoice and find a surprise.

Why we exist

The best idea in the room rarely wins. The first one on the register does. We built Stackly so that the people doing the actual building never lose that race for want of a lawyer who picks up the phone.
